# 一个简单的调查表

创建一个用户友好的调查表，用于收集来自用户的各种类型的信息。该表单将包括不同类型的输入字段，例如文本框、单选按钮、复选框和提交按钮。重点是创建清晰、可访问且易于使用的表单布局。

---

# A simple survery form

A user-friendly survey form that collects various types of information from users is created. The form will include different types of input fields, such as text boxes, radio buttons, checkboxes, and a submit button. The focus is on creating a clear, accessible, and easy-to-use form layout.
